Ingredients

  • Some random frozen pizza idk
  • Essence of Soggy

Steps

  1. Take the pizza out of the box and remove any plastic wrappers
  2. Remove any cardboard base from underneath, or not if you like the taste of wet cardboard
  3. Read the box to get the cooking time and temperature
  4. Ignore the instructions to use a preheated oven and wack the pizza in right away
  5. Wait the amount of time stated on the box
  6. Remove your most likely soggy pizza from the oven and enjoy
