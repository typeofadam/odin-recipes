Toast

We all love a good piece of Toast right?
An english staple with a very complicated recipe, get ready folks.

Ingredients

  • Nice Bread
  • Wonderful Butter (non-negotiable)
  • Anything else your heart desires

Steps

  1. Slice bread if not already sliced
  2. Put as many slices as bread as you want in the toaster
  3. Once the toaster has launched your bread into the stratosphere, place it on a plate tenderly
  4. Get a knife and put almost too much butter on
  5. Marvel at your latest accomplishment
  6. Add any extra toppings
